Basketball selection meetings announced

Feb. 17

The Iowa Print Sports Writers Association has announced the dates of their first all-state selection meetings for girls and boys basketball. We appreciate the support of the Iowa Basketball Coaches Association in helping us with venue space to select our first teams.

We will meet:

• Girls Basketball: Saturday, March 3 at 9 a.m.
• Boys Basketball: Saturday, March 10 at 9 a.m.

Both meetings will be held at the Comfort Inn, near Wells Fargo Arena. This is the same site as basketball all-state meetings have been held at during recent years. The format of the meetings will also be the same as in prior years, minus a couple minimal changes.
All coaches and print sports writers in Iowa are invited and welcomed to participate.
