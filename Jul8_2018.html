Nominations for baseball all-state

Sunday, July 8, 2018

Coaches, athletic directors and sports writers may nominate players for this year's Iowa Print Sports Writer Association's All-State Baseball teams. If you did not receive a password, email iowasportswriters@gmail.com to receive it.
By providing an email address, you'll receive a link which will allow you to return to any nomination at a later date to update it as necessary.
All nominations must be submitted or edited by Monday, July 23, 2018 at 5 p.m.
